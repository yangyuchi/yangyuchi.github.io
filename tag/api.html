My thoughts about Data Science

- Data Science is all about solving business problems with data, no matter with what kind of tools/techniques.
- Get hands dirty. Things only gets clearer after 10000 times of trial and errors.
- Accept imperfection. There ain't no such thing as a free lunch, no tool/package/algorithm is perfect. Try to understand the current scenario and choose the simplest solution that meets the requirement.
- There's no such definition of Data Scientist. A Data Scientist is a problem solver. Keep expanding your skill set, because you don't know when you need to make a demo with HTML/Javascript, build backend APIs to serve a model, or develop CI/CD pipelines to automate workflows. Yes, being "full stack" is never gonna be easy, but it's also a lot of fun :)
